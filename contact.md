# Contact

The best ways to reach me:

## GitHub

Most of my work lives on GitHub. Feel free to open an issue, send a PR, or just say hi.

[github.com/maint](https://github.com/maint)

## Email

For anything that doesn't fit a GitHub issue, you can reach me by email. Replace `[at]` with `@`:

`maint [at] github.com`

*(Writing it this way to reduce spam.)*

---

I try to respond within a few days. If it's urgent, mention that in your message.
